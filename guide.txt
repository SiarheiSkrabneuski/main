# EPAM Website User Guide

## Homepage

The homepage of EPAM website provides an overview of their services, industries, insights, and more. It includes sections such as:

- **Main Navigation**: Contains links to Services, Industries, Insights, About, Careers, and Contact Us.
- **Featured Stories**: Highlights case studies and reports.
- **Customer Results**: Showcases the impact of EPAM's solutions.
- **Partners**: Lists technology partners.
- **News**: Displays recent news and updates.
- **Footer**: Contains links to various policies, social media, and subscription management.

## Navigation

### Services

To explore the services offered by EPAM, click on the "Services" link in the main navigation.

### Industries

To learn about the industries EPAM serves, click on the "Industries" link in the main navigation.

### Insights

To read insights and reports, click on the "Insights" link in the main navigation.

### About

To know more about EPAM, click on the "About" link in the main navigation.

### Careers

To explore career opportunities, click on the "Careers" link in the main navigation.

### Contact Us

To get in touch with EPAM, click on the "Contact Us" link in the main navigation.

## Footer Links

The footer contains links to various policies, social media, and subscription management.

- **Investors**: Information for investors.
- **Open Source**: Details about EPAM's open source contributions.
- **Privacy Policy**: EPAM's privacy policy.
- **PRC Privacy Policy**: Privacy policy for PRC.
- **Cookie Policy**: Information about cookies used on the website.
- **Applicant Privacy Notice**: Privacy notice for job applicants.
- **Web Accessibility**: EPAM's web accessibility statement.
- **UK Modern Slavery Statement**: Statement regarding modern slavery.
- **Recruitment Fraud Disclaimer**: Disclaimer about recruitment fraud.
- **Code of Ethical Conduct**: EPAM's code of ethical conduct.

## Social Media Links

EPAM is active on various social media platforms. The footer contains links to their profiles on LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram, and YouTube.

## Subscription Management

Manage and customize your email subscription preferences by clicking on the link in the footer.

## Additional Information

EPAM's website also includes sections for news, featured stories, and customer results, showcasing their impact and collaborations with various partners.

## Contact Information

For further inquiries, you can visit the "Contact Us" page linked in the main navigation.
